Lasagna

Description

Layers of flat sheets of pasta filled with cheeses, ground beef, and covered in a tomato sauce

Ingredients

  • Sheets of pasta
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato based meat sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer ingredients
  5. Bake at 350 degrees for one hour
