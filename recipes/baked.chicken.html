Baked Chicken

Description

Chicken breast baked with seasonings

Ingredients

  • Boneless and skinless chicken breast
  • Butter or olive oil
  • Salt
  • Seasonings(paprika, garlic powder, salt and black pepper)

Steps

  1. Prepare chicken breast by washing and drying them
  2. Brine chicken breast in lukewarm water with a touch of salt for roughly 15 minutes
  3. Coat chicken with butter or oil and apply seasoning mix
  4. Baked chicken in the oven at 450 degree for roughly 15 minutes
