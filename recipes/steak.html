Grilled Steak

Description

Grilled steak with charred outside, seasoned to perfection, with a warm pink center

Ingredients

  • 12 oz. boneless ribeye or New York strip steaks
  • 2 table spoons of olive oil or butter
  • Salt, pepper, and garlic salt

Steps

  1. About 25 minuted before grilling, remove steaks from refrigerator and coat with oil or butter, and then add seasonings
  2. Heat grill to high
  3. Grill steaks for about 4-5 minutes until lightly brown and charred, and then flip steaks to continue grilling for roughly 3-5 minutes for medium rare
  4. Finally, remove steaks and wrap in foil to rest for about 5 minutes
